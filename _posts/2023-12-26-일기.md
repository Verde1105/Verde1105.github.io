## MySQL 계정 생성 쿼리[use mysql]
```
CREATE USER 'verde'@'%' IDENTIFIED BY '1234';

GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES ON *.* TO 'verde'@'%' IDENTIFIED BY '1234' WITH GRANT OPTION;
```
